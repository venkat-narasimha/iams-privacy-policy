Request Account Deletion - IAMS Mobile

How to Request Account Deletion

To delete your IAMS Mobile account and associated data:

  1. Email us at: ads@harithatech.com
  2. Subject: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include your registered email address in the email body
  4. We will process your request within 30 days

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • User account information (name, email, profile data)
  • Authentication credentials
  • User preferences and settings
  • Associated app data stored in your account

What Data May Be Retained

  • Attendance Records: Retained for up to 7 years as required by educational institution policies and legal compliance (FERPA, institutional retention policies)
  • Anonymous Analytics Data: Aggregated, anonymized usage statistics that cannot identify you
  • Legal/Compliance Records: Data required by law, legal proceedings, or institutional audit requirements

Retention Period

Educational records including attendance logs are retained for up to 7 years after account deletion as required by institutional policies and legal obligations (FERPA compliance).

For questions about data deletion, contact: ads@harithatech.com
