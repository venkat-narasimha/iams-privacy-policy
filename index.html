Privacy Policy for IAMS Mobile

Last updated: 24-oct-2025

1. Introduction

IAMS Mobile ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Account Information: Name, email address, student/faculty ID, role (Admin, Faculty, Student, Parent)
  • Authentication Data: Login credentials and authentication tokens
  • Profile Information: Department, course information, contact details

2.2 Attendance Data

  • Biometric Data: Fingerprint templates (stored securely on device/server)
  • Attendance Records: Check-in/check-out times, location data
  • Device Information: Device ID, IP address, device model

2.3 Technical Information

  • Device Data: Device model, operating system, app version
  • Usage Data: App interactions, feature usage, session duration
  • Network Information: IP address, network type, connection status
  • Crash Reports: Error logs and performance data (via Sentry)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Authentication: Verify user identity and manage access
  • Attendance Tracking: Record and manage attendance data
  • App Functionality: Provide core app features and services
  • Analytics: Improve app performance and user experience
  • Security: Detect and prevent fraud or unauthorized access
  • Communication: Send notifications and updates

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Secure Storage: Data encrypted using industry-standard encryption
  • Local Storage: Some data stored securely on your device
  • Server Storage: Data stored on secure cloud servers
  • Access Controls: Role-based access to sensitive information

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Educational Institution: Your school or organization for attendance management
  • Service Providers: Third-party services for app functionality (Expo, Sentry)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect rights

6. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Expo: App development and deployment platform
  • Sentry: Error tracking and performance monitoring
  • Google OAuth: Authentication services (handled by backend)
  • CloudFront: Content delivery and configuration

7. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access: Request access to your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Portability: Export your data in a readable format
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics or notifications

8. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while account is active
  • Attendance Records: Retained as required by educational policies
  • Analytics Data: Retained for up to 2 years
  • Logs: Retained for up to 1 year for security purposes

9. Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for educational use. If your target audience includes children under 13, additional protections apply under COPPA. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

10. International Users

If you are using our app from outside the United States, please note that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States where our servers are located.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.
